pad thai
Description:
pad thai is the street food of thailand and boiii is it wonderful.
Difficulty:
hard
Duration:
1d4h
Cuisine:
Asian
Ingredients:
  • 2 cups of honey
  • 5 cups of milk
  • 5 tbsp of jelly
  • 6 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 1 tsp of butter
Instructions:
  1. Add butter to spread
  2. Churn Jelly with peanut butter
  3. Blend honey with milk
